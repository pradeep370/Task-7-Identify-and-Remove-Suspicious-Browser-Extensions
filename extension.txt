üö® Suspicious Extension Analysis ‚Äì Download Manager (S3)
   
     Purpose: Manage and organize downloads in the browser.  
     Reason for Suspicion: Requests overly broad permissions that could be exploited for malicious purposes.

     Permissions Granted & Risks:
  
     1. Access data for all websites ‚Äì Could read/alter sensitive info like passwords or bank data.
     2. Download files & modify history ‚Äì Can track or manipulate downloads.
     3. Open downloaded files ‚Äì Risk of executing malicious content.
     4. Access browsing history ‚Äì Enables complete activity tracking.
     5. Display notifications ‚Äì Could send phishing-like popups.
     6. Access browser tabs ‚Äì Allows monitoring of all tabs and content.

     Action Taken: Removed from browser to prevent potential misuse.  
       Risk Level:  High


‚úÖ Trusted Extension Analysis ‚Äì Dark Reader

     Purpose: Enables dark mode on all websites to reduce eye strain and improve readability at night.  
     Reason for Permissions: Required to modify website styles in real-time and to know which tabs to apply changes to.

     Permissions Granted & Risks:
     
     1. Access data for all websites ‚Äì Needed to change site colors; in malicious extensions, could alter or   steal information.
     
     2. Access browser tabs ‚Äì Identifies which sites are open to apply styling; in malicious cases, could track browsing activity.

     Action Taken: Kept installed ‚Äì verified open-source and reputable.  
     Risk Level: Low (trusted source)
